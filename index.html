About Us

CPIAR.org represents a research project led by Dr. Festus Amadu at Florida Gulf Coast University. Climate Policy Innovation for Adaptation and Resilience (CPIAR) explores the potential impact of AI and Machine-Learning applications on the adoption of Climate Smart Agriculture practices.
